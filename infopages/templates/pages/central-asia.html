Why is it profitable to invest in Central Asia.

Central Asia, a region east of the Caspian Sea that includes Uzbekistan, Kazakhstan, Tajikistan, Kyrgyzstan and Turkmenistan, has significant potential to become an attractive investment destination. Compared to other emerging economies, Central Asia is receiving less attention from potential investors so far. Its potential in attracting foreign direct investment (FDI) is estimated at up to $ 170 billion, including $ 40-70 billion in non-resource sectors, over the next 10 years.
